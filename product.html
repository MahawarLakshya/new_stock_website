Coconut Shell Powder

Coconut shell powder is obtained by grinding & pulverising of the Coconut Shells. Coconut shell powder is a beneficial soil conditioner which improves soil structure, retains water and absorbs nutrients. It is also helpful in controlling the pH value of soil. It is widely used as a substrate in horticulture and agriculture for seed germination and plant growth. The nitrates and nitrites formed in air due to lightning are absorbed by this soil through water or air as medium. Coconut shell powder, when mixed in proportion with lime effectively stabilizes expansive soil, increases dry density and reduces its swelling and shrinking. Due to such properties of coconut shell powder, it supports organic farming & natural fertilisers when added to soil helps plant growth effectively.
